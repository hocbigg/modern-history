# Other curricula

This curriculum is one of a few efforts to inform learners how they can pursue Modern History independently. Below are a few other such efforts.

- [OER Project: Modern History Courses](https://www.oerproject.com/) (Free, standards-aligned online Modern History curricula with Big History and Origins to the Present)
- [Educating for American Democracy Roadmap](https://www.educatingforamericandemocracy.org/the-roadmap/) (Inquiry-based framework for history and civics, adaptable for modern US history focus)
- [Big History Project](https://www.bighistoryproject.com/home) (Free course linking history to broader scales, including modern era)
