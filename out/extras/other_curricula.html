Other curricula

This curriculum is one of a few efforts to inform learners how they can pursue Modern History independently. Below are a few other such efforts.

  • OER Project: Modern History Courses (Free, standards-aligned online Modern History curricula with Big History and Origins to the Present)
  • Educating for American Democracy Roadmap (Inquiry-based framework for history and civics, adaptable for modern US history focus)
  • Big History Project (Free course linking history to broader scales, including modern era)
